ABOUT THE ACADEMY

A Complete Football Development Environment

Minerva Football Academy is a professional player-development institution committed to identifying, training and preparing talented footballers for elite competition. Its structured environment combines technical coaching, tactical education, physical development, discipline, education, residential support and competitive exposure.
