Start from the dashboard to review your scouting activity, assigned matches, saved players and recent reports. Use the sidebar to move between players, matches, academies and reporting tools.

Open Scout Dashboard

Open the Players page, use the search bar and filters to narrow results, then select a player profile. Use the save control to add the player to your personal scouting watchlist.

Browse Players

Open Create Report, select the relevant player and match, complete each evaluation section and save the report as a draft or submit it when your assessment is complete.

Create Report

Draft reports can be updated before submission. Submitted reports may become read-only depending on platform review rules and backend permissions configured by the administrator.

View My Reports

Open Matches, select a fixture and use the save control. Saved matches remain available in your scouting activity for quick access and future reporting.

View Matches

Use the Academies page to search by location, type, programme, rating and verification status. You can open detailed profiles, save academies and recommend new institutions.

Browse Academies

Open My Profile from the sidebar. Update your name, organization, experience, location, scouting preferences and contact information, then save your changes.

Open My Profile

Confirm that your email and password are correct, check your internet connection and try signing in again. Use the password recovery option when available or contact support for account verification.
