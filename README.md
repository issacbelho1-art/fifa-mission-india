# 🇮🇳 Bharat Football Fans
## Mission FIFA 2034

A modern football platform dedicated to supporting India's journey toward the FIFA World Cup 2034 by connecting players, coaches, academies, scouts, supporters, and football organizations through one digital ecosystem.

---

## 🌟 Vision

**One Nation. One Dream. India to the World.**

Our mission is to discover talent, develop football at the grassroots level, and unite the Indian football community under one platform.
