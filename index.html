Knowledge Centre

All Questions

12 questions

Mission FIFA 2034 is an independent digital football initiative designed to connect players, coaches, academies, scouts and supporters across India. Its goal is to improve talent visibility, access to opportunities and football participation.

General

No. Mission FIFA 2034 is currently an independent football development initiative and is not officially affiliated with FIFA, AIFF or any government body unless an official partnership is announced in the future.

General

Players, parents or guardians, coaches, academies, scouts, clubs, schools, volunteers, supporters and organisations interested in Indian football development will be able to participate.

General

After registration becomes available, players will be able to add their playing position, age group, preferred foot, location, match experience, achievements, photos and approved football videos.

Players

Minor players may be allowed to create profiles only through a parent or verified guardian. Their contact details, location and personal information should never be publicly exposed without appropriate consent and safeguards.

Players

No. Creating a profile does not guarantee selection, trials, contracts or academy admission. The platform is intended to improve visibility and access to verified opportunities, while final decisions remain with the relevant organisations.

Players

Academies will be able to submit their organisation details, location, registration documents, coaching information, programmes, facilities and official contact details for review.

Academies

Verification means that basic submitted information and documents have been reviewed. It does not guarantee the quality of coaching, safety, facilities or services. Families should still conduct their own checks before joining any programme.

Academies

Verified scouts may eventually search public player profiles using filters such as age group, position, location and experience. Access to sensitive information should remain restricted and carefully monitored.

Scouting

The production platform should require identity, organisation and professional-role checks before granting scouting access. Suspicious accounts or requests should be reportable and reviewed by administrators.

Scouting

Sensitive data should remain server-controlled and should never be trusted solely because it comes from the client. Access controls, secure authentication, limited public fields and audit logs should be implemented before launch.

Safety and Privacy

Do not make payment based only on a message or selection promise. Verify the organisation independently, preserve screenshots and report suspicious activity. The platform should never claim that payment guarantees selection.
