Player Story

About the Player

Arjun began playing football at a young age and quickly developed a passion for attacking play. His ability to control the tempo, identify passing opportunities and operate between defensive lines has made him a promising midfield talent.

He currently trains with Delhi Football Academy, where he continues to improve his technical, tactical and physical abilities. His long-term goal is to represent India and compete at the highest professional level.
