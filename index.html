01 / THE MISSION

THE WORLD CUP DREAM STARTS BEFORE KICK-OFF.

Talent exists in every village, school, street and football ground across India.

Mission India calls for a focused youth football programme that brings together scouting, elite coaching, sports science, competitive exposure and national support.

The purpose is simple: give talented Indian children the training, environment and belief needed to compete confidently against the best young footballers in the world.

02 / THE ROADMAP

FROM THE STREETS TO THE WORLD STAGE.

A clear and practical development pathway can transform raw talent into a competitive national youth team.

PHASE 01

NATIONAL TALENT SEARCH

Scout promising players through schools, academies, district tournaments, open trials and video applications.

PHASE 02

REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT CAMPS

Bring selected players into regional camps for technical, tactical and physical assessment.

PHASE 03

ELITE NATIONAL CAMP

Develop the strongest players in a centralised professional training environment.

PHASE 04

INTERNATIONAL EXPOSURE

Play against strong youth teams with different footballing styles and competitive systems.

PHASE 05

COMPETE WITH BELIEF

Enter the world stage with discipline, preparation, courage and a winning mentality.
