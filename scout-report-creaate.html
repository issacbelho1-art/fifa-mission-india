SCOUTING STANDARDS

Professional Reporting Guidelines

Every scouting report contributes to identifying India's future football talent. Ensure your observations remain objective, accurate and evidence-based.

Observe Carefully

Evaluate technical skills, movement, decision-making, communication and overall impact throughout the entire match.

Remain Objective

Avoid personal bias. Base every rating on measurable performance and consistent observations.

Support Your Ratings

Provide clear written explanations that justify the numerical scores and recommendations.

Maintain Confidentiality

All reports remain confidential and should only be shared within FIFA Mission India scouting operations.
